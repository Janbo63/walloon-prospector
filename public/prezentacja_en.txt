SLIDE 2: THE TARGET AUDIENCE & UNTAPPED REVENUE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[Title]: Our Guests: Untapped Border-Zone Potential
[Content]:
The Sudeten area and the Świeradów-Zdrój resort attract over 1.2 million tourists annually. The goal of this partnership is to bring these visitors directly to Krobica:
* German Tourists: Traditionally the largest group of visitors in the region, seeking history and wellness.
* Czech Tourists: A rapidly growing segment from just across the nearby border.
* English Speakers: A growing global market, currently almost entirely untapped due to a lack of translated tours.
[Bottom Note]: 
The lack of foreign-language trails and simple automated bookings means the mine loses a significant portion of these visitors. Our goal: increase visitor numbers and boost revenues.
[Visual]: 
Shows the historical map of the Walloon Trail connecting Orłowice and Krobica.

SLIDE 3: PHASE 1 — OPENING THE DOORS (IMMEDIATE IMPACT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[Title]: Phase 1: Opening the Doors (The Phone App)
[Content]:
We will launch a simple mobile app named "Walloon." It runs instantly when scanning a QR code (no App Store/Google Play download required):
* 4-Language Audio-Guide: Visitors hear the mine's history on their phones in Polish, German, Czech, and English.
* Frictionless Tours: Foreign guests join standard tours led by the Polish guides and hear translations in real-time.
* AI Rock Scanner (Walloon's Eye): Kids and adults take photos of minerals on the heaps, and the phone identifies the stone and displays local legends.
[Visual]: 
Shows a high-fidelity smartphone mockup displaying the AI rock scanner.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 4: PHASE 2 — BOOKINGS & ONLINE MERCHANDISE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[Title]: Phase 2: Booking Convenience and Online Souvenirs
[Content]:
We expand the Krobica brand by introducing simple digital services to increase sales:
* Online Ticket Purchasing: A simple, automatic online booking system. Customers pay in advance, and the system auto-manages guide group limits.
* Online Souvenir Store: Online sales of cassiterite samples, cobaltite shales, and locally tumbled stones.
* Post-Visit Purchase Funnel: A tourist who scans a raw stone during their visit can order a polished, tumbled version in the app to be shipped directly to their home.
[Visual]: 
A diagram showing: 1. Online Ticket booking -> 2. Rock Scanning -> 3. Post-visit polished stone delivery.
